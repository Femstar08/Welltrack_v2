WellTrack ("we", "our", "the app") is a health and fitness application that helps users optimise their recovery and training through data-driven insights. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your personal information.

1. Data We Collect

1.1 Account Data

  • Email address (used for authentication)
  • Display name (entered during onboarding)
  • Date of birth, height, weight, biological sex, activity level (entered during onboarding for calorie/macro calculations)

1.2 Health & Fitness Data (via Health Connect / HealthKit)

With your explicit permission, WellTrack reads the following health data from your device:

Data TypePurpose
StepsTrack daily activity and set movement goals
Heart Rate & Resting Heart RateMonitor recovery and calculate training readiness
Heart Rate Variability (HRV)Assess nervous system recovery
Sleep SessionsOptimise rest and adjust tomorrow's plan
Weight & Body FatTrack body composition progress toward goals
Exercise Sessions & CaloriesPersonalise workout plans and calorie targets
DistanceMeasure running and walking activity
VO2 MaxTrack cardiorespiratory fitness trends
Oxygen Saturation (SpO2)Monitor blood oxygen levels

We never write data back to Health Connect. WellTrack only reads health data with your permission.

1.3 User-Entered Data

  • Meals & Nutrition: Food items logged, meal plans, macro targets, pantry inventory
  • Workouts: Exercise sessions, sets, reps, weights, training plans
  • Habits: Habit tracking entries and streaks
  • Supplements: Supplement protocols and daily logs
  • Goals: Fitness and body composition goals with target values
  • Bloodwork: Lab test results including reference ranges (optional)
  • Reminders: Notification schedules for meals, workouts, and supplements

1.4 Sensitive Health Data

WellTrack optionally collects sensitive wellness metrics as part of the daily check-in:

  • Morning erection (yes/no) — a general wellness indicator
  • Weekly erection quality (1-10 scale) — tracked for hormonal health awareness

These fields are:

  • Completely optional — skipping them does not affect app functionality
  • Marked as sensitive in our database and excluded from AI processing by default
  • Only included in AI-generated insights if the user explicitly enables the "Vitality Data Sharing" toggle in Settings
  • Never displayed in shared views, exports, or logs

1.5 Third-Party Integrations

If you connect Garmin or Strava:

  • We store OAuth access tokens encrypted on our server (never on your device)
  • We receive health metrics (stress, sleep, VO2 max, activities) via webhook push notifications
  • You can disconnect at any time from Settings, which revokes access and deletes stored tokens

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Recovery Score Calculation: Your health metrics are processed using a deterministic mathematical formula to generate a daily recovery score (0-100). No AI is involved in the calculation.
  • Training Plan Generation: Your recovery score, goals, and biometrics determine workout intensity and calorie targets using rule-based logic.
  • AI Narrative Insights: An AI model explains your trends in plain English. The AI receives aggregated health summaries — never raw personal data. AI responses are suggestive ("consider...") and never prescriptive ("you must...").
  • Meal Planning: Calorie and macro targets are calculated from your biometrics and activity level, then used to generate meal suggestions.

We never sell your data. Your health information is used solely to provide the WellTrack service.

3. Data Storage & Security

  • Backend: Data is stored in a Supabase (PostgreSQL) database with Row-Level Security (RLS) enabled on all tables — you can only access your own data
  • Encryption in transit: All API calls use HTTPS/TLS
  • Token storage: OAuth tokens for Garmin/Strava are stored encrypted server-side
  • Local storage: Sensitive session data uses flutter_secure_storage (Android Keystore / iOS Keychain)
  • Offline data: Cached data stored locally using Hive (encrypted box for sensitive fields)
  • AI processing: AI calls are server-side only — no AI model runs on your device. Health data sent to AI is aggregated and anonymised.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal health data with third parties except:

  • Supabase: Our database hosting provider (data processor, not controller)
  • AI Provider (Anthropic/OpenAI): Receives anonymised health summaries for narrative generation only — no personally identifiable information is sent
  • Garmin/Strava: Only when you explicitly connect these services, and only to receive your data back into WellTrack
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose data if required by law or to protect user safety

5. Your Rights

5.1 Access & Export

You can view all your data within the app. Contact privacy@welltrack.fit for a full data export in machine-readable format.

5.2 Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time:

  • In-app: Settings > Delete Account
  • By email: Contact privacy@welltrack.fit

Account deletion permanently removes all your data from our servers. Some data may take up to 30 days to fully propagate through backup systems.

5.3 Consent Withdrawal

You can revoke Health Connect permissions at any time via your device's Settings > Health Connect > WellTrack. You can disable AI data sharing toggles in the app's Settings screen. You can disconnect Garmin/Strava at any time.

5.4 Data Portability

Under GDPR and similar regulations, you have the right to receive your data in a structured, commonly used format. Contact us to request this.

6. Children's Privacy

WellTrack is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If you believe a minor has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

7. Data Retention

  • Active accounts: Data retained for the lifetime of the account
  • Deleted accounts: All data purged within 30 days of deletion request
  • Anonymised analytics: Aggregate, non-identifiable usage statistics may be retained indefinitely for service improvement

8. Cookies & Tracking

WellTrack is a mobile application and does not use cookies. We do not use advertising trackers, analytics SDKs, or fingerprinting technologies.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated via an in-app notification. The "Last Updated" date at the top of this page indicates when the policy was last revised.
